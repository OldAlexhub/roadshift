# Feature Graphic Notes

**Dimensions required:** 1024 x 500 px (PNG or JPEG)

## Recommended Design

**Background:** Deep dark navy or near-black (#0a0e1a)

**Visual elements:**
- City grid lines in soft blue/cyan (#1e3a5f or #2a4a7f) forming a glowing street network
- RoadShift wordmark in large bold white/blue text centered or left-aligned
- Subtitle: "Traffic Puzzle" in smaller text below
- Small glowing vehicle icons (cab, van) positioned on grid lines as if in motion
- Subtle color-coded route lines (blue for compact cab, green for van, purple for access van)
- Optional: Small star icons in gold to suggest the rating system

**Color palette:**
- Background: #0a0e1a
- Grid lines: #1e2d45 or #1e3a5f
- Accent glow: #3b82f6 (blue), #f59e0b (amber)
- Text: #f1f5f9 (near white)
- Stars: #fbbf24 (gold)

**Typography:**
- "RoadShift" in a bold, modern sans-serif (weight 800+)
- "Traffic Puzzle" in a lighter weight below

**Mood:** Nighttime city grid, calm authority, strategic gameplay

## Tagline option for feature graphic:
"Plan routes. Move riders. Beat the grid."

## Notes:
- Do not use real city names, logos, or agency branding
- Do not use any imagery suggesting real emergency services
- The graphic should clearly convey a puzzle/strategy game aesthetic
- Avoid overly complex layouts; keep it readable at small sizes
