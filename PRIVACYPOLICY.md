## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Old Alex Hub handles information in connection with the RoadShift: Traffic Puzzle mobile application for Android.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

RoadShift does not require you to create an account. We do not collect, transmit, or store on our own servers any personal information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Physical address
- Location
- Photos or camera content
- Contacts
- Financial or payment information

---

## Local Gameplay Data

Gameplay progress, settings, and achievements are stored locally on your device using Android local storage (AsyncStorage). This data includes:

- Level completion and star ratings
- Best scores per level
- Achievement status
- Sound and haptic settings
- Tutorial completion state
- Ad eligibility counters and timestamps used to enforce respectful ad frequency

This data does not leave your device through the RoadShift app. It can be deleted at any time by using "Reset All Progress" in Settings, by clearing the app's data through Android settings, or by uninstalling the app.

---

## Advertising

RoadShift is free to play and is supported by advertising through **Google AdMob**. The Google Mobile Ads SDK is integrated into the app and may collect and process data to deliver and measure advertisements. This may include:

- Device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
- Device information and settings
- IP address
- Ad interaction data

This data collection is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA where applicable.

**Consent:** If you are located in a region where advertising consent is required, a consent dialog will appear when you first launch the app. You can revisit your ad privacy choices at any time through Settings > Ad Privacy Options.

---

## Internet Access

The app uses internet connectivity for advertising purposes only. All gameplay, level progression, scoring, and achievements function fully offline without an internet connection.

---

## Permissions

RoadShift requests only the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `android.permission.INTERNET` | Required to load and display ads |
| `android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` | May be required by the Google Mobile Ads SDK |

The app does not request permissions for location, camera, microphone, contacts, storage, notifications, Bluetooth, phone, calendar, biometrics, or any other sensitive capability.

---

## Data Deletion

All RoadShift gameplay data is stored only on your device. To delete it:

1. Open RoadShift and go to **Settings > Reset All Progress** to clear gameplay data while keeping the app installed.
2. Go to your Android device **Settings > Apps > RoadShift > Clear Data** to delete all app data.
3. **Uninstall** the app to remove all local data.

For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's data deletion options in their privacy policy.

## Children's Privacy

RoadShift is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is not configured for child-directed treatment under applicable advertising policies.

## Backend Services

Old Alex Hub does not operate a server or backend service for RoadShift. No gameplay data is transmitted to our servers. Third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) may process data under their own privacy policies as described above.
